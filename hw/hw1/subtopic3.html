Depending on your internet usage and budget there are many options when selecting an anti-virus software package. There are users who are naturally cautious about their web-browsing and information sharing. For these people, a simpler or more economical solution may be sufficient. Other uses require high speed internet browsing and response time. If you are one of these, you may want to find a package that minimizes the impact on using system resources yet still provides a sufficient level of service. For most people, the solution is in the middle. Looking for something economical that still provides a wide range of protection without impacting system response time to heavily is ideal.
